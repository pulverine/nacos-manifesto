C stands for Cultivating Professional Career Development: I will personally initiate contact with potential partners and oragnisations in my network through professional emails, phone calls, or in-person visits. O stands for Organization of Excursions and field trips: I will utilize the connections and relationships I have with external professionals in order to plan regular visits to their companies, research institutions, and innovation hubs to expose students to real-world applications of their skills.

D stands for Development of Facilities: I will conduct a comprehensive assessment of the existing facilities available to NACOS FTU members and identify areas that require improvement or additional resources to enhance the overall experience. I will also solicit feedback from NACOSITES to understand their specific needs and preferences regarding facilities.

E stands for Establishing Internship Placements for our students: I will personally develop and maintain strong relationships with local and regional tech companies, startups, and organizations and establish effective communication channels between NACOS FTU and these tech companies. I will also create a comprehensive database of potential internship opportunities, categorizing them based on industry, duration, and required skills.

C stands for Conducting Extra-curicula tutorial sessions: We will identify qualified students or external professionals who can serve as tutors for the extra-curricular sessions. We will also ceate a resource hub or library where students can access additional learning materials.

R stands for Reinforcing Sporting Activities: I will work along with the sports director and form a dedicated committee responsible for organizing and overseeing sporting activities within the chapter. We will also develop a sports calendar outlining a schedule of regular sporting events throughout the academic year. This could include intramural leagues, friendly matches, and an annual NACOS FTU Sports Day

A stands for Advocating for Mentorship Programs: I will foster collaboration with alumni, industry professionals, and faculty members who are willing to volunteer their time as mentors. and reach out to local tech communities and organizations to expand the pool of potential mentors.

F stands for fostering External Collaboration and Networking: We will send invitations to professionals from various sectors of the tech industry to conduct talks and webinars. Partner with industry experts to organize workshops and training sessions that enhance the technical skills of NACOS FTU members.

T stands for thriving in Social Events: I will work with the social director to plan a diverse range of social events to cater to the varied interests of the members. This can include game nights, parties, dinner nights and community-building activities.

S stands for Skill development: I will work along with the school management and department to identify and integrate emerging technologies and tools into the coursework to ensure relevance. I will work together with the other executives to organize regular workshops and webinars covering a spectrum of technical skills, ranging from programming languages to software development to cybersecurity skills, and so on.
